# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in OpenVersus a respectful and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background or experience level.

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**
- Being respectful and constructive in all interactions
- Welcoming contributors of all skill levels
- Accepting feedback gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community as a whole

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, insults, or personal/ad hominem attacks
- Trolling and/or deliberately disruptive behavior
- Publishing others' private information without consent

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers via GitHub Issues or the community Discord server. Maintainers reserve the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this Code of Conduct, and to ban contributors who engage in unacceptable behavior.
